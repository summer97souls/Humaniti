About Us

  • Humaniti is a Bangalore-based NPO committed to creating a better future for everyone, focusing on both human and animal welfare.
  • The organization provides opportunities for impoverished children and works to ensure that every animal has a home.
  • Composed of young visionaries, Humaniti aims to make equality and culture accessible to all, regardless of economic status.
  • The NPO supports underserved communities through programs in education, healthcare, and economic development.
  • Humaniti collaborates with local partners, volunteers, and donors to address pressing social issues and create sustainable change.
  • The organization's mission is to uplift and inspire, building a more inclusive and compassionate society where every individual can thrive.
  • Humaniti's initiatives not only provide immediate relief but also equip individuals and communities with tools for long-term success.
  • A strong emphasis is placed on advocacy, with efforts to raise awareness about systemic challenges contributing to inequality.
  • Humaniti engages with policymakers, communities, and stakeholders to drive positive change and advance its vision of a just and equitable world.

A Government School Excursion

Humaniti had talked on its social media handles, about how life skills are of paramount importance, in order to thrive in student and adult life. Well, we just didn't talk about it, we taught them too. We arranged a visit to a nearby government school , and organised interactive activities through which we taught them imperative life skills, in their native language. The feedback was astounding, and the children shared an amazing time with us.

A Paw-Some Day

The quality of humanity, is not just about humans. It's also about animals that accompany us. Our team's visit to a dog shelter for volunteering, was filled with paw-sitive vibes, and lots of fun.

An Entreprenuer's Dream

Education about entrepreneurship is crucial for encouraging future entrepreneurs! We taught entrepreneurial skills via establishing and using skill sets used in building our NPO Humaniti. This was an initiative taken by NEP, and an opportunity given to us by our school.
